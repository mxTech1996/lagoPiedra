# **App Name**: PeñalverPro

## Core Features:

- Homepage with Business Overview: Showcase the company's mission, expertise, and value proposition with a compelling hero section.
- Service Catalog: Detail all administrative and consulting services offered with clear descriptions and benefits.
- Product Listing and Purchase: Display digital products for sale with details and pricing, including templates and guides.
- Dynamic Case Studies: Highlight successful client projects with interactive case studies showcasing quantifiable results using animations
- Client Testimonials: Incorporate a rotating carousel of client testimonials to build trust and credibility.
- Contact Form: Enable potential clients to submit inquiries through a contact form.
- AI-Powered Consulting Tool Recommendation: The AI will analyze client needs and suggest tailored consulting services using the products and services available. The AI is a tool, whose decisions should always be checked.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark navy blue (#002D62) for trustworthiness and professionalism.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F0F0) for a clean and modern look.
- Accent color: Teal (#008080) for CTAs, icons, and highlights, evoking growth and innovation.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif font for a clean, modern aesthetic.
- Card-based design with consistent spacing and clear visual hierarchy.
- Subtle hover animations and transitions to enhance user engagement and provide feedback on interactions.
- Professional and relevant icons to represent services and products.
